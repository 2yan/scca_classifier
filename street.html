Street Class Questions

Is your steering wheel within 1 inch of standard size?

Are your fenders unmodified?

Are your tires 200 treadwear and DOT legal?

Are your wheels stock width?

Is your wheel offset ±7mm (±0.275") from a standard wheel for the car?

Is your wheel diameter ±1" from the standard wheel for the car?

Do you have the stock number of wheel bolts or studs?

Are your shocks at most adjustable in two ways? Cars that have shocks that are more adjustable from the factory are permitted.

Is your suspension geometry unmodified?

Are your brake lines stock? If your car is 1992 or older you can have solid/braided lines.

Does your car meet the anti-roll bar restrictions?

Rule: Substitution, addition, or removal of a single anti-roll bar and supporting hardware (brackets, endlinks, bushings, etc.) is permitted. The use of any bushing material is permitted. A bushing may be implemented as a bearing.
